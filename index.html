About

个人简介Biography

张和贵，博士，副教授，硕士生导师。研究兴趣主要聚焦于图学习与复杂网络分析，尤其关注多重网络表示学习、时序图表示、跨平台社交网络影响力最大化以及智能营销场景中的算法建模。

Hegui Zhang is an Associate Professor and Master's supervisor. His research interests lie in graph learning and complex network analysis, with particular emphasis on multiplex network representation learning, temporal graph representation, influence maximization in cross-platform social networks, and algorithmic modeling for intelligent marketing scenarios.

近年来主持国家自然科学基金青年项目、辽宁省自然科学基金博士科研启动项目、辽宁省教育厅高校基本科研项目等，参与多项国家重点研发计划、国家自然科学基金重点国际合作项目和国家社会科学基金重大项目。

In recent years, he has served as principal investigator for the NSFC Young Scientists Fund, the Liaoning Provincial Natural Science Foundation Doctoral Start-up Project, and the Basic Scientific Research Project of the Liaoning Provincial Department of Education, while also participating in multiple national key R&D and major research programs.

学术定位Academic Focus

面向复杂社交网络与数据智能场景，结合图表示学习、结构角色建模与网络动力学，探索更具解释性、迁移性与应用价值的计算方法。

His work aims to develop interpretable, transferable, and application-oriented computational methods by integrating graph representation learning, structural role modeling, and network dynamics for complex social networks and data-intelligent settings.

复杂网络分析Complex Network Analysis

关注复杂社交网络、耦合网络、多层网络中的结构规律、传播机制与行为建模。

Focus on structural patterns, diffusion mechanisms, and behavior modeling in complex social, coupled, and multilayer networks.

影响力最大化Influence Maximization

面向跨平台社交网络与竞争传播场景，研究关键传播者识别与最优传播策略。

Study of key influencer identification and optimal diffusion strategies in cross-platform and competitive propagation settings.

智能商务Intelligent Business

将机器学习与网络分析方法应用于智能营销、信用评估、商业预测等实际问题。

Application of machine learning and network analysis to intelligent marketing, credit evaluation, and business prediction.
